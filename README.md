## Overview

VectorJ is a student-centric platform built to help aspiring developers discover opportunities, collaborate with peers, and accelerate their professional growth.

Students often rely on multiple disconnected tools for networking, hackathon participation, project collaboration, learning resources, and career development. VectorJ brings these experiences together into a single ecosystem where students can connect with like-minded individuals, participate in hackathons, manage projects, share knowledge, and showcase their achievements.

The platform also enables organizations, communities, and educational institutions to engage with students through events, challenges, and collaborative initiatives, creating a bridge between talent and opportunity.

The platform runs at [vectorj.in](https://vectorj.in) with a React SPA deployed on Vercel and an Express API backed by MongoDB Atlas.

## Features

### 👨‍🎓 For Students

* Discover and explore hackathons, competitions, and community events.
* Build a professional profile showcasing skills, achievements, and experiences.
* Connect with peers, collaborators, mentors, and like-minded developers.
* Participate in project-based communities and networking opportunities.
* Track personal growth, activity, and engagement across the platform.

### 🏆 For Event Organizers

* Create and manage hackathons from a centralized dashboard.
* Publish event details, timelines, eligibility criteria, and rewards.
* Monitor registrations and participant engagement.
* Build communities around events and initiatives.
* Connect directly with student participants.

### 🏫 For Educational Institutions

* Establish institutional profiles and showcase campus initiatives.
* Increase student participation in hackathons and innovation programs.
* Connect students with external opportunities and industry events.
* Strengthen engagement within the student developer ecosystem.

### 🤝 Community & Collaboration

* Real-time messaging and networking.
* Community-driven discussions and knowledge sharing.
* Collaborative project planning and task management.
* Resource discovery and learning support.
* Personalized notifications and activity updates.

### 📊 Insights & Growth

* Track participation across events and activities.
* Monitor networking and community engagement.
* Visualize progress and skill development.
* Gain insights into learning and collaboration trends.
